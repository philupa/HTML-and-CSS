Noodles is my cat. He is about 19 years old and about 6kg. We like to give him different names depending on the kind of cat he is being at the time. I made this page to help you find the name I would give you, if you were Noodles.

To find your Noodle Name, all you need is the first letter of your first name and the date of the month you were born. Find these in the lists below, and put them together to get your full title.


This is not a phishing scam, although Noodles does love tuna.
